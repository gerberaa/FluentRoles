🍳 Fluent Role System

In our community, everyone has a chance to become a true Chef! Join activities, show your creativity, and earn unique roles. Below are all possible roles and the conditions for obtaining them:

✅ @Verified Chef

  • How to get: Pass the Captcha verification.
  • Benefits: Access to main community channels. The first step to active participation.

😹 @Meme Chef

  • For: True meme lords!
  • How to get: Weekly meme battle (best meme contest). Post your meme in 🦄｜memes. The best works are selected by the team, and their authors receive the @Meme Chef role.
  • Tip: Want to become a meme legend? Show what you can do!

🎥 @Content Chef

  • For: Creators of quality content.
  • How to get: Write an article, make a Twitter thread, or shoot a video about Fluent. Content must be valuable, original, and high-quality. Submit your material to ✍｜content-submissions.
  • Note: Superficial or weak content will not be considered.

🧑‍🏫 @Helping Chef

  • For: Those who love helping newcomers.
  • How to get: Actively communicate in Discord. Help other users navigate. Contribute to a friendly atmosphere.
  • Attention: Spam does not count and may reduce your chances. Real help should be sincere and useful.

🎨 @Artist Chef

  • For: Creative souls.
  • How to get: Create a song, draw a picture, show any talent (poems, animations, etc.). We are looking for unique artistic expressions — don’t hold back your imagination!

👑 @Ultimate Chef

  • For: The most active and creative members.
  • How to get: Earn several other Chef roles. Consistently demonstrate activity, creativity, and loyalty. Be a role model for other members.
  • Benefit: This is the most prestigious role — recognition of your contribution to the Fluent community.

Actively and passionately — and you will become a true Ultimate Chef!
